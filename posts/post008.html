1813. Sentence Similarity III

Medium Problem

April 8, 2021 Shiyun Zhang

Problem Analysis

Two sentences sentence1 and sentence2 are similar if it is possible to insert an arbitrary sentence (possibly empty) inside one of these sentences such that the two sentences become equal. 

From the above definition, we know:

  1. If sentence1 equals to sentence2, then these two sentences are similar because we can insert empty inside sentence1 or sentence2
  2. If any word in sentence1 not in sentence2 or any word in sentence2 not in sentence1, these two sentences are not similar
  3. If words in sentence1 in the middle of sentence2, these two sentences are not similar
