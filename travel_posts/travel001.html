Holland Ridge Farms

A pristine flower farm, bursting with millions of rainbow blooms

April 27, 2021 Shiyun Zhang

If you want to enjoy the scenery of the sea of ​​tulips, Holland Ridge Farms, a pristine flower farm, bursting with millions of rainbow blooms, is the right place to go. I am sure the first word that comes out of your mouth is ‘WOW’ the moment you arrive at the farm. 

The tulip blooming period will last until early May. If you want to go to the farm, the middle of April will be the best time. Before going to the farm, you can buy tickets on Holland Ridge Farms’ homepage. 

The moment I arrived at the farm, millions of tulips were dancing in the wind in front of me. The farm was endless, and so was the flower sea. The view was stunning! Even though it was a weekday, the farm was still full of people. We lined up to enter the farm. I can see the smiles and expectations on people's faces. Girls were wearing beautiful dresses and straw hats, families were taking pictures, kids were running around. I love this kind of excitement.

There were so many places to take pictures. The blue sky, the endless rainbow flower sea, the colorful butterfly… Also, in the north of the farm, there was a small animal farm. Parents can go with their kids and have fun with the horses, donkeys, goats, alpacas and bulls.
